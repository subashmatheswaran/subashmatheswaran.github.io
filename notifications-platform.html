Notifications Platform - User Experience

Designing a notification management system to send important updates to Jamaican Citizens.

Role UX Designer
Platform Web

Overview

The Notifications Platform is a web application that allows Jamaican Government officials to create, send and manage important updates and notifications to thier Jamaican citizens via email, SMS and WhatsApp.

The Problem

Jamaican Government officials were struggling to send important updates like hurricane alerts to thier citizens.

  • Hard to create and send notifications in bulk.
  • Hard to categorize and target certain group of Jamaican citizens.
  • Hard to schedule notifications in different platforms to bulk target Jamaican citizens.

Research and Discovery

Natural hazards are a significant challenge for Jamaican citizens. Sending updates and notifications like hurricane alerts are a differentiators from life and death.

Caption: Impact of natural hazards on Jamaican citizens.

💡 Key Finding

Jamaican citizens are not getting updates and notifications during natural hazards on time.
